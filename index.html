Turn Bluetooth on

In order for us to configure your clock we will need to send some information to it via Bluetooth.

Please make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your mobile device and then run through the following steps to turn the clocks Bluetooth mode on

  1. Turn your clock on
  2. Wait till the light on the front of your clock is either red or green
  3. Now press and hold the demo button on your clock
  4. Bluetooth mode is enabled once the green light starts flashing
  5. Go to next step on you app

Please enter your wifi SSID number and password, these can be found on your wifi router.
